Education

I am currently a first-year Computer Information Science (CIS) student at Ozarks Tech, and I’m expected to graduate in 2026. This marks the beginning of my second degree journey, after deciding to shift from electronics engineering. My first degree, which I pursued from 2021 to early 2024, was unfortunately left unfinished due to a series of personal challenges, including a major relocation across countries. Although I had to stop my engineering studies, I’ve gained invaluable life experiences that have fueled my determination to pursue my true passion in the tech world.

Throughout my time as an electronics engineering student, I developed a strong foundation in technology, but as I explored the rapidly evolving fields of computer science and information systems, I found myself captivated by the limitless potential of tech innovations. My fascination with technology only grew stronger as I saw its transformative impact on various industries and everyday life. This has motivated me to fully immerse myself in CIS, where I hope to combine my technical knowledge with my creative problem-solving abilities to contribute meaningfully to the digital landscape.
